About Forktune

Forktune is your playful recipe companion!

Don’t know what to cook? Just let Forktune pick something delicious for you.

Features

  • Get a random recipe when you can’t decide what to make
  • Save favorites locally — no sign-ups, no cloud, just on your device
  • Share and save recipes from your favorite cooking sites directly into the app

Whether you’re bored of your go-to meals or love experimenting in the kitchen, Forktune helps you find your next favorite dish — one lucky spin at a time!

If you have any questions, you can contact us at app.forktune@gmail.com.
